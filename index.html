About Me

I'm Alex Morgan, a freelance photographer and digital artist based in the Pacific Northwest. My work is driven by a passion for capturing the intersection of light, emotion, and storytelling.

With over a decade of experience, I specialize in portrait, landscape, and architectural photography, while also exploring the boundaries between traditional photography and digital art. Each project is an opportunity to create something unique and meaningful.

Whether you're looking for stunning portraits, breathtaking landscapes, or innovative digital artwork, I bring a meticulous eye for detail and a commitment to excellence to every collaboration.
